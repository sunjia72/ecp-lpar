\]
Multiplying,
\[
\frac{\sin(x+y)}{\cos^2x\cos^2y}

=\frac{\tan^2 y-\tan^2 x}{\sin h}.
\]

\[
a_n=\sum_{k=1}^{n-1}\frac{\tan^2(kh)-\tan^2((k-1)h)}{\sin h}

\]

\[
a_n=\frac{\cot^2 h}{\sin h}=\frac{\cos^2 h}{\sin^3 h}.

\[
\frac{a_n}{n^3}

\[
n^3\sin^3 h \to \left(\frac{\pi}{2}\right)^3,
