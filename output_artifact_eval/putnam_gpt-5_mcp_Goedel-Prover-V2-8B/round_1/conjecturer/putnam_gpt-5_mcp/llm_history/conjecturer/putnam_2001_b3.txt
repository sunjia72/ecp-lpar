Now for the exact computation:

For each integer \(m\ge 1\),
\[
\operatorname{round}(\sqrt n)=m
\]
exactly when
\[
m^2-m+1 \le n \le m^2+m.
\]
(These are the integers between \((m-\tfrac12)^2\) and \((m+\tfrac12)^2\).)

So the terms with \(\operatorname{round}(\sqrt n)=m\) contribute
\[
(2^m+2^{-m})\sum_{n=m^2-m+1}^{m^2+m}2^{-n}.
\]
This geometric sum is
\[
\sum_{n=m^2-m+1}^{m^2+m}2^{-n}
=2^{-m^2+m}(1-2^{-2m}),
\]
hence the \(m\)-th block contributes
\[
(2^m+2^{-m})2^{-m^2+m}(1-2^{-2m})
=2^{-m^2+2m}-2^{-m^2-2m}.
\]
Rewrite this as
\[
2^{1-(m-1)^2}-2^{1-(m+1)^2}.
\]
Therefore
\[
S=\sum_{m=1}^\infty \left(2^{1-(m-1)^2}-2^{1-(m+1)^2}\right),
\]

\[
S=(2^{1-0^2}+2^{1-1^2})-\lim_{M\to\infty}\bigl(2^{1-M^2}+2^{1-(M+1)^2}\bigr)=2+1=3.
