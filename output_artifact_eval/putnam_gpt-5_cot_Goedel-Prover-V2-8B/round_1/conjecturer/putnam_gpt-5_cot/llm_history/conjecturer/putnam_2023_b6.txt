\[
ra+cb=n \qquad (1\le r,c\le n).
\]
So \(S=(s_{r,c})\).

Key simplification:

- Subtract the \(n\)-th row from every previous row, and the \(n\)-th column from every previous column.
- For \(r,c<n\), the new \((r,c)\)-entry becomes
  \[
  s_{r,c}-s_{r,n}-s_{n,c}+s_{n,n},
  \]
  which is exactly the number of **positive** solutions of \(ra+cb=n\).
