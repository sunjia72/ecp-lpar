In any allowed move, we swap adjacent inverted entries.

- If the swap does **not** involve \(4\), then \(I(\pi)\) stays the same.
- If the swap **does** involve \(4\), then \(4\) swaps with exactly one element that is on the wrong side of it, so \(I(\pi)\) decreases by exactly \(1\).
